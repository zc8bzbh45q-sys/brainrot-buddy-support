Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 12, 2025

Summary: We respect your privacy. We don't collect personal information, don't store your conversations on our servers, and don't share your data for marketing. Your chats are processed by OpenAI for AI responses and stored only on your device.

Introduction

This privacy policy applies to the Brainrot Buddy mobile application (the "App") for iOS devices. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information.

OpenAI Processing

Local Storage
